Runway incursion figures from 2023 seem to reinforce the idea that air travel has been getting less safe. According to CBS, there have been 571 runway incursion incidents in 2023 to date, from 1,732 in 2022.

While it may be tempting to attribute the increased frequency of runway incursions to the lack of flying hours brought about by the Covid-19 pandemic, the reality is that runway incursions occurred less frequently in the years following the pandemic than before, pointing to traffic numbers as the top determinant, as suggested by a report by Transport Canada.
