The Most Misunderstood Source of Electricity
December 6, 2023

Masters capstone project completed as part of the Media Innovation Studio 2 course under the direction of Professor Dan Zedek. I developed text mining workflows to scrape text data from news outlets to determine how news media coverage of nuclear energy affects its public perception. My analysis of the resulting corpus revealed a lack of emotional resonance, suggesting a balanced emotional approach to nuclear energy. Academic literature and expert sources suggested that this neutrality was a deliberate editorial choice, making news coverage a unsuitable proxy for measuring public sentiment towards nuclear energy.
