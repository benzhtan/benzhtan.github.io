  • December 6, 2023
    The Most Misunderstood Source of Electricity
    Masters capstone project done in Media Innovation Studio 2 under the direction of Professor Dan Zedek. I developed text mining workflows to scrape text data from news outlets to determine how news media coverage of nuclear energy affects its public perception. My analysis of the resulting corpus revealed a lack of emotional resonance, suggesting a balanced emotional approach to nuclear energy. Academic literature and expert sources suggested that this neutrality is deliberate, making news coverage a poor proxy for measuring what the public thinks about nuclear energy.

    Expanding the circle: The reality of implementing restorative justice across the country
    Class-wide project led by Prof. Dan Zedek for Media Innovation Studio 1 course as part of the Media Innovation and Data Communication program at Northeastern University's School of Journalism.
